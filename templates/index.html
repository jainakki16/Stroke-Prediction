What is Stroke ?

A stroke happens when the blood vessels in the brain either become clogged or burst, leading to loss of functionality of our body parts.

Stroke is the leading cause of premature death and in every 4 minutes someone dies of a stroke but upto 80% of stroke can be prevented if silent stroke is diagnosed early.
